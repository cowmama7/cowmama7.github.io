The Emerson TrainDex

The TrainDex is a place for me to organize all of the trains
I've ever been on in my life. As a train aficionado, I take pride
in the number of trains I've been on. Below you will find a table with
images of the respective trains, as well as links to their wikipedia pages.
You can find a lot more information about these trains on their respective
wikipedia pages.

I've been on trains in both the USA, as well as Europe. I've rode them for function
as well as for fun! My personal favorite trains to ride are the Amtrak Silver service
trains, with the viewliner rolling stock, as they're the most comfortable trains I've been on.
My favorite locomotives are the new Siemens Charger locomotives that have just started service
on several amtrak routes.
