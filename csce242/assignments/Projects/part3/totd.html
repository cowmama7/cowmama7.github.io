Siemens Charger

The new Siemens Charger Locomotives have been rolled out across Amtrak's network since 2022, and are nearly all in revenue service. With a top speed of 125 mph, and the ability to run in electric or diesel modes, nothing is stopping these trains from taking on the vastness of the United States.
